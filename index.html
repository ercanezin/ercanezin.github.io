WELCOME

Here is a bio of myself

Ercan Ezin completed his Ph.D. in Context-Aware Recommender Systems from the University of Bristol in May 2024. He holds an MSc in Advanced Computer Science from the University of Essex, UK, and dual BSc degrees in Computer Education and Instructional Technologies and Software Engineering from Bahçeşehir University, Türkiye.

Dr. Ezin's research focuses on novel AI and Machine Learning research projects, particularly in Context-Aware Next-POI Recommendations and, more recently, Natural Language Processing. With extensive experience as an AI Researcher and Teaching Assistant at the University of Bristol, he has published papers in top-tier conferences on recommender systems and is actively involved in Natural Language Processing projects in a newly established research lab called ANLAM Lab.

Dr. Ezin's industry background includes roles as a Java Software Developer, Data Scientist, and IT Director, bringing a blend of academic expertise and practical software development skills to his work. Dr. Ezin has been appointed as a tenured faculty member at Harran University, Türkiye.
